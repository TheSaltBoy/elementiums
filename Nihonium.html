Nihonium

Nihonium is a myterious and mostly unknwon element, named after the country of Japan.

Symbol Atomic Number Atomic Weight
Nh 113 286
Category Group Period
Post-transition Metal 13 7

About

Not much is known about Nihonium. It's named after the Japanese word for Japan, Nihon, meaning "Sun Origin".

Nihonium isn't found anywhere in nature, and very little of it has ever been made.

Uses

Research is still ongoing for this element. It's highly radioactive and doesn't last very long (it's half life is only ten seconds), so it's hard to research.

History

Nihonium was discovered relatively recently, in 2003 in Russia by a collaboration of Russian and American scientists.
